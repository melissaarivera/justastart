Development Opportunities

Throughout my co-op at Just A Start, I've taken in how the organization operates and the impact it has on the community. I've observed the importance of data management with community engagement efforts, and I've come up with an approach to enhance the way we collect and process data to optimize our outreach.

Community Outreach Pipeline Proposal

  1. Research: Identify organizations in nearby communities that seem to target similar demographics or have overlapping missions. Input these organizations into our Salesforce database, along with specific affiliated contacts and their contact information. Indicate the status of our relationship with this organization.
  2. Reach out: Email an affiliated contact to introduce our organization and schedule a time to meet to discuss potential collaboration. Include pdfs of our flyers and links to our website.
  3. Visit Location: Deliver flyers in-person, table, conduct information sessions for locals.
  4. Data Collection Add specific location to inquiry form as option of "how you heard about us." Ask participants during info sessions & verify during testing if possible to ensure accurate data, and update Salesforce.
  5. Analysis: Utilize data (inputted in Salesforce) to create reports indicating the outcomes of outreach from different cities and types of organizations (i.e. libraries compared to community centers, etc.) reaching the most people.
  6. Adjust Approach:Draw conclusions from data to adjust which organizations we focus on partnering with. Keep information in Salesforce database under the respective Organization.
