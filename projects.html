Salesforce Data Cleanup

I worked with Jude Alfaro, the Salesforce Administrator at Just A Start.

Goal of this project

Our organization utilizes Salesforce to manage all of our data. After over 30 years of the Adult Career Training Programs, this data has accumulated and the methodology in which we track information needs to be analyzed. Various information from participants is collected through various forms/objects, and my job was to ensure there is consistency between the field names.

Community Outreach Expansion

Goal of this project

Our aim is to develop partnerships with organizations in Malden, Medford, and Arlington to expand our reach.

I researched organizations and developed a list of contacts to reach out to. As a result, Just A Start has formed partnerships with the following:

  • Medford Public Library
  • Medford Family Network
  • Malden OSPCD
  • Malden Public Library
  • Arlington Robbins Library
  • Housing Corporation of Arlington
