Salesforce Data Cleanup: Part 3

Relating Program Engagement and Employment Records of Alumni

I created a table from exported Salesforce data, filtered to include only people that went through our programs (stage=graduated/withdrawn/terminated) and only post-program employment records (entry_type≠pre-prog/in-prog employment). Then, I applied a formula to only include ones with start dates within 6 months of each other. In the second sheet labeled For_delete I only outputted contacts in recruitment stages (stage≠graduated/withdrawn/terminated) and with entry_type=pre-prog employment, so it only shows contacts that never made it to the program and their employment records. I used power queries (merge) to get both sheets, and we can now remove employment records unrelated to our program participants.
